Debra Janssen President & CEO Debra Janssen is president and chief executive officer of CDS Global. She previously served as chief operating officer from 2012 to 2014. Debra has more than 25 years of experience working with customer data, security and high-volume transaction processing. She joined CDS Global from MasterCard Worldwide, where she was group executive of integrated processing solutions and managing director of Access Prepaid. Prior to that, Debra was senior vice president of electronic payment processing and software services for Fidelity National Information Services, and she previously served as president of debit services and the STAR Network at First Data Corporation. Earlier in her career, she held executive positions at Hallmark Cards, Deluxe Corporation and Metavante Corporation. In 2013, Iowa Governor Terry Branstad appointed Debra to the board of the Iowa Partnership on Economic Progress, a bipartisan advisory council focused on creating jobs, increasing family incomes and enlarging the state’s gross product. She received her liberal arts degree from the University of Wisconsin.
Malcolm Netburn Chairman Malcolm Netburn is chairman of CDS Global. Since joining the company in February 2007, Netburn has provided thought leadership to the various industries served by CDS Global and has been a featured speaker at conferences throughout these industries. Netburn has more than 25 years of experience with magazine, newspaper, book and media companies. Before joining CDS Global, he was a founding partner of Netburn McGill, a New York-based consulting firm that he helped establish in 1986. A graduate of The George Washington University, Netburn previously served in executive roles at Chemical Week Associates, Hart Publications, the Christian Science Monitor, the National Wildlife Federation and Scholastic. He was the 2013 inductee into the Fulfillment Management Association Hall of Fame.
Clint Crain Vice President, Brand Extensions Clint Crain is vice president of brand extensions at CDS Global. In this role, he works to develop new opportunities to extend customer brands, expand the CDS Global brand into new products, services and other revenue channels, and grow CDS Global’s nonprofit business. Nancy Gessmann Senior Vice President, Global Operations Nancy Gessmann is senior vice president of global operations for CDS Global. In this position, Nancy is responsible for the company's operations facilities, contact centers, data capture locations, lettershops and distribution centers across the U.S., Canada, U.K. and Australia. Paul Polus Chief Financial Officer Paul Polus is chief financial officer at CDS Global.
